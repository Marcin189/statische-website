Sint-Jan Church (Sint-Janskerk)

Why visit the Sint-Jan Church?
The Sint-Jan Church is one of Waalwijk’s most iconic landmarks. This beautiful neo-Gothic church, built in the early 20th century, is famous for its impressive architecture and colorful stained-glass windows. It’s a peaceful place where history, art, and spirituality come together. Visiting the Sint-Jan is a must for anyone who wants to experience the cultural heart of Waalwijk.

Waalwijk City Center

Why visit the City Center?
Waalwijk’s city center is a lively area full of shops, restaurants, and cafes. It’s the perfect place for a relaxing day out, whether you want to enjoy a coffee, do some shopping, or simply walk around the cozy streets. The combination of modern buildings and historic charm makes it a great place for both locals and visitors to explore.

Lido Waalwijk

Why visit Lido Waalwijk?
Lido Waalwijk is a popular outdoor swimming pool surrounded by greenery, perfect for warm summer days. It offers large pools, water slides, and plenty of space to relax or enjoy a picnic. Families, friends, and sports enthusiasts all love spending time here. It’s a great spot to cool off, have fun, and enjoy the outdoors in Waalwijk!
